Frequently asked Questions

How do I access all videos and downloadable content on BusyBeavers.com?

Become a Paying Subscriber to BusyBeavers.com to Stream Watch all our Videos in a Kid-Friendly Closed platform. You can also Download our Songs, Worksheets, Flash Cards and Textbooks. The cost is $20 for 3 Months Unlimited Access. If you're not already a member, click the Login button above to start your subscription!

How much does it cost to become a Subscriber?

It cost $20 USD for 3 Months of Unlimited Access to our Videos and Downloads. This Subscription is recurring and will auto-renew at the end of 3 months however, you can cancel anytime and not be billed again.

What if I want to Cancel my Subscription?

You are free to Cancel your Subscription anytime. Your account will remain active and you will have access to the site until the end of your 3 month period. Then your password will expire and you can no longer log in. Do it yourself by logging into PayPal or send us an email requesting cancellation and we'll do it for you. If you wish to re-start your Subscription. It's easy to do that any time in the future.

I have a subscription, but forgot my password?

If your subscription is active, but you have forgotten your password, you can use our password recovery tool. Click the Login button above and then click "Forgot password". Please note that you will need to provide the email address that you used to sign up for a BusyBeavers.com subscription.
